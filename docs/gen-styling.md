# Cascadify Genaral Styling


## Background
`<div class="bg-<color></div>`

### the colors are: black, cyan, white, red, yellow, green, orange, blue and purple

## States


`<div class="lock"></div>` this will be lock the element, the user cannot interact.

`<div class="hide"></div>` this will hide the element.


## Resize

`<div class="resizeable></div>` there is also 'resizeable-vertical' and 'resizeable-horizontal'

## Cascadify Theme

You can add `theme="green-code"` (there are also dark, nature, ocean) to any element, and it will be "themed"
